Joint Environmental Statement from the Pope and Ecumenical Patriarch Bartholomew I of the Orthodox Church

Dave Randle

Dear Friends
Not too many years ago it would have been hard to imagine a joint environmental statement coming from the Pope and the leader of the Orthodox Church. However, in an increasing age of interfaith cooperation, many are realizing that global partnerships and increasing moral leadership for the environment is one of the best hopes we have to move toward a more sustainable society.

Although words are not actions, they are a start and certainly more than governments gave us in Bali at the recent World Summit on Sustainable Development Prep-Com meeting.

Let's work together to build a stronger interfaith coalition for Global healing. Be Well, -- Dave

This version of the site has been restored in 2021 through careful extracts from the Wayback Machine with substantial stylistic reformating for consistency. Reacting to religious fanaticism and the challenges of advancing and sustaining a more equitable civilization, a global interfaith movement has sprung from the grassroots of religion and spirituality. InterfaithNews.Net (INN) seeks to chronicle this movement by focusing primarily on positive interfaith and religious news, events, and resources.
